Welcome to the E-Vocation Wizard!

E-Vocation wizard is a tool that aims to help you find a new/alternate career path. You will be asked to choose up to two occupation/vocation categories. As a follow-up, you will be asked to rate some occupations from the categories you chose.

First of all, you have the option to fill some minimal information about yourself. This will help our algorithms improve in the long run!

Finally, you will be redirected to a screen that displays your custom results, based on our Machine Learning models.
