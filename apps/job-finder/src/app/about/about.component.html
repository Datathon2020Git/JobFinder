About Us

E-vocation Wizard is a cooperative idea. As a group of friends, many of us were constantly struggling to find our place in the job market. By extension, we thought it would be awesome if there was an online tool that could help everyone facing this and similar issues in some kind of way.

And Thus the Wizard Was Born

After Spiros came across the EU Datathon competition, he managed to convince George about participating and after some brainstorming we realised he idea was already in front of us. Based on ESCO’s occupations and skills groupings and CEDEFOP’s forecasts, we created a Recommender App that suggests potential career paths for everyone, based on what they like! Spiros and Giannis created various machine learning models for grouping and then recommending occupations, while utilizing dynamic inputs from any user of our platform. The Wizard is just doing its first steps in the world, just as we do as well.

E-Vocation Wizard is just the start of what’s about to come. We plan on implementing our own Search Engine for Job Openings, utilizing data from as many (valid) websites we can get. In addition, we intend to provide dynamic and easy to use graphic visualizations for the DECEFOP forecasts that will dynamically change as CEDEFOP provides more data.

We deeply understand that seeking a job in your country and abroad is a stressful situation and we want to help with that. There are people that lose their jobs in the blink of an eye, or people that struggle with rejection or people that are just starting their career and feel that they have to figure their way out, swimming through a complete chaos that is the job market.
Many of us have pulled through, we hope that many more do as well.
