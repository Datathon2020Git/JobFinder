About Us

E-vocation Wizard is a cooperative idea. As a group of friends, many of us were constantly struggling to find our place in the job market. We thought it would be awesome if there was an online tool that could support anyone facing similar issues.

And Thus the Wizard Was Born

After Spiros came across the EU Datathon competition, he managed to convince George about participating. After some brainstorming we realised the idea was already in front of us. Based on ESCO’s occupations and skills groupings, combined with CEDEFOP’s forecasts, we created a Job Recommendation Application that suggests potential career paths for everyone, upon fulfilling a simple form. Spiros and Giannis created various machine learning models for grouping and then recommending occupations, while utilizing dynamic inputs from our users.

The Wizard is just doing its first steps in the world, just as we do as well.

E-Vocation Wizard is just the start of what’s about to come. We plan on implementing our own Search Engine for Job Openings, utilizing data from as many professional communities we can get. In addition, we intend to provide dynamic and easy to use graphic visualizations for the DECEFOP forecasts that will change as they provide more data.

We deeply understand that seeking a job in your country and abroad is a stressful situation and we want to help with that. For many of us the job market still is a complete chaos.
Many of us have pulled through, we hope that many more do as well.
